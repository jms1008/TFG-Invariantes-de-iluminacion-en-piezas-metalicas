TFG: Lighting Invariants in Metal Parts

Author: Jonás Martínez Sanllorente

Tutor: D. Carlos Lopez Nozal

Co-tutor: D. Pedro Latorre Carmona

This application is designed to apply different invariant transformation methods on images containing metal parts to demonstrate an improvement in subsequent part identification using image grouping methods.
You will be able to select both sample images and your own images.
Different invariant transformation methods.
Different image grouping methods.
Save the corresponding results.
